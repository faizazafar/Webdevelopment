About Us

Shazz Salon is all about creating a new look and treating your imperfections beautifully to make you feel young, stylish and flawless. That is why a wide range of treatments is offered by the salon that includes Hair treatments, Skin treatments, Eye treatments and Hand and Feet treatments. Hair treatments include hair cut, low lights, coloring, and perming keratin treatment. All these services are exceptionally splendid and up to date that is sure to make your hair look exquisite and gorgeous.
